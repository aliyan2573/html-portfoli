About Us

Since 1988, Aliyan Autos has been a trusted name in the motorcycle industry, driven by a passion for creating affordable, stylish, and high-performance bikes for riders across Pakistan. What started as a small family-owned shop has grown into a brand synonymous with quality, reliability, and innovation.

Our Motorcycle Collection

CD70cc

The ultimate affordable and reliable bike, perfect for daily commuting. Known for its fuel efficiency and durability, it’s a favorite among riders who value practicality.

Pridor 100cc

A blend of comfort, efficiency, and style, the Pridor is designed for those who want a smooth and stylish ride without compromising on performance.

CG 125cc

The dream of every youngster, this bike is available in both kick-start and self-start versions. The self-start variant comes in two stunning finishes: silver and gold, offering a touch of elegance to its powerful performance.

CB 125 Dream

A stylish and powerful motorcycle with a 125cc engine, designed for riders who want a perfect balance of performance and aesthetics.

CB 150

Our most premium offering, the CB 150 is known for its power, style, and unmatched comfort. It’s the ultimate choice for riders who demand the best in performance and design.
